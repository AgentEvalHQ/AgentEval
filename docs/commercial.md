# Commercial & Enterprise

> **Status:** No commercial offerings are being sold today.  
> This page describes **planned** options and how to engage if your team wants to help shape them.

## Open-source commitment

AgentEval's core will remain:

- **MIT licensed**
- **publicly developed**
- **community-driven**

We do **not** plan license changes or retroactive restrictions.

### Contribution policy (Model 1: inbound = outbound)

Contributions to the open-source repositories are accepted under **MIT**.

Any future commercial work (if it exists) will be maintained **separately** and will **not** be created by copying community contributions into proprietary code.

## Enterprise interest

Some organizations have asked about:
- paid support / enablement
- enterprise-grade integrations
- packaged, curated content

If your team wants to discuss requirements or become an early design partner, please reach out.
